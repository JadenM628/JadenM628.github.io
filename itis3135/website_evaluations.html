Website Evaluations

Apple

This is a well known technology company that originally started with just computer but has expanded into many other product lines.

  • Contrast: The main colors of Apple's website is Black and White. They use those to make their products pop on the page with its crisp colors on the products.

  • Repetition: Apple tends to repeat blocks of black or white with their products in them. It makes it easy to navigate and find or explore their products.

  • Alignment: The alignment of the page is mostly centered for each respected element. Apple is going for a simplicity look with the centered text.

  • Proximity: Links embedded in the website are strategically placed for the consumer to learn more or purchase a product.

Craigslist

This is a known website that is used for listing and viewing local advertisements and sales.

  • Contrast: The main colors of Craigslist are White, Purple, and Grey. The use white for the background, purple for the text, and grey to sort elements on the page.

  • Repetition: They have links all colored to blue but change to purple once visited. All sections of the website have grey backgrounds.

  • Alignment: Alignment is kind of off because of the different sizes of the list. Margins seem off on top and bottom.

  • Proximity: Links on website seem clustered as most links are close together on a list.
