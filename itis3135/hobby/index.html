Where to Buy

There are different types of packs and boxes of cards you can buy. They all have different price points. If you are looking for a single card you can purchase them on the aftermarket.

Here are some of the options to buy sports cards and the price ranges:

  • Single Packs: $2-$10
  • Retail Box: $20-$60
  • Hobby Box: $100-$500

You can purchase cards from several different retails such as Walmart, Target, etc. Or you can purchase direct from the manufacturer via their website.
