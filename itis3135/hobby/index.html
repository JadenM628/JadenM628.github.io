Where to Buy

There are different types of packs and boxes of cards you can buy. They all have different price points. If you are looking for a single card you can purchase them on the aftermarket.

Here are some of the options to buy sports cards and the price ranges:

  • Single Packs: $2-$10
  • Retail Box: $20-$60
  • Hobby Box: $100-$500

You can purchase cards from several different retails such as Walmart, Target, etc. Or you can purchase direct from the manufacturer via their website.

Understanding What to Look For

There are all different types of desgins and concepts of sports cards out there. It could be overwhelming to see all the different types of cards. Most of the time it will tell you what different types of cards you could possibly get in the box. If you do some research about the product you bought you can figure out if you got a rare card or not.
